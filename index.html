About

I am an Electrical Engineer with 5+ years of experience in Energy sector, mainly in Electricity Distribution Operations and Regulations.

Now, however, I have decided to pursue a career in a field where I can express more of my creative, problem solving and analytical skills. That is why I decided to teach myself programming, focusing on web development with Javascript and its ecosystem. My aim with this is to get hired as a web developer and to make valuable contributions to open-source projects. I have been learning from various resources like FreeCodeCamp.org, Udemy courses, FrontEndMasters and various books and online blogs.

I can comfortably make front-end projects with React & its ecosystem, using either vanilla CSS (CSS Grid/FlexBox), preprocessors like Sass, or CSS frameworks like Bootstrap and Bulma, and Node/Express back-end with MongoDB as database. I can also make small to medium-scale apps with Vue and Elm.

Currently I am working on FreeCodeCamp certifications and learning Vue, Elm, MobX, user authentication, PostgreSQL, Webpack & Typescript. I also code in Haskell and Python in my spare time.
